# Waycliffe Estates verification

## Findings

Waycliffe Estates is a named residential subdivision/neighborhood in the Ballwin–Manchester area of St. Louis County, Missouri. It is not a city, municipality, or separate mailing address.

The St. Louis County Recorder of Deeds provides an official subdivision-name plat search, although its interactive lookup was blocked by CAPTCHA during this review. A Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices property-search page explicitly classifies the area as **Subdivision: Waycliffe Estates 1** in Ballwin. An independent neighborhood-search page identifies **Waycliffe Estates, Manchester**. Realtor.com search results for 1210 Miremont Drive also identify the property as located in the Waycliffe Estates neighborhood, but the full page was inaccessible in the research browser.

Because the name is real but unfamiliar to many buyers, the website should explain it as the home's subdivision rather than using it as unexplained marketing copy.
